Why "Labs"?

Because everything here starts as an experiment — and the good ones don't stay that way. These apps began as small ideas built quickly, then grew into full products shaped by the families who use them every day. The name keeps us honest about how we build: fast, in the open, listening. No ads, no tracking, ever. Use every app without an account, or sign in with one free Ogbara Labs account to sync your progress across every app and device — your data stays yours.

📲Real PWAs. Install to your home screen from the browser.
✈️Offline first. Once loaded, they work without internet.
☁️Sync when you want. One free account backs up progress across all apps and devices.
