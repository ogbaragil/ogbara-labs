Why "Labs"?

Because everything here starts as an experiment — a small idea built quickly, played with the people it's for, and polished until it feels right. No accounts, no ads, no tracking. Your data stays on your device.

📲Real PWAs. Install to your home screen from the browser.
✈️Offline first. Once loaded, they work without internet.
🔒Private by design. Progress is saved on your device only.
